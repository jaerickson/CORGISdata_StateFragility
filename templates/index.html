About the State Fragility data

This data and research about state fragility was taken from a CORGIS data set. It applies a score from -10 to +10. The scores are for a country's security, politics, economics, and social aspects. The data is only for countries with a population of over 500,000. The data is used to evaluate a countries fragility. The fragility is a measure of how susceptible a country is to a crisis. This is for internal and external shocks to the country. The data can be used to assist countires in need or to determine strong countires.

sources http://www.lse.ac.uk/internationalDevelopment/research/crisisStates/download/drc/FailedState.pdf http://www.systemicpeace.org/inscrdata.html
